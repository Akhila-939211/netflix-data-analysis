# Netflix Data Analysis Report

## Introduction
This project analyzes Netflix dataset using Python.

## Objective
To find insights like Movies vs TV Shows, Top Countries, Top Genres, and Content trends.

## Analysis
- Movies vs TV Shows distribution
- Top 10 countries
- Top 10 genres
- Content added over years

## Insights
- Movies are more than TV Shows
- USA has highest content
- Drama is most common genre
- Content increased after 2015

## Conclusion
This project helps understand Netflix data trends.
